# Sentiment-Analysis
Sentiment Analysis examines the problem of studying texts, like posts and reviews, uploaded by users on microblogging platforms, forums, and electronic businesses, regarding the opinions they have about a product, service, event, person or idea. Sentiment analysis or opinion mining refers to natural language processing (NLP).It is the process of analyzing the text to determine the emotional tone they carry.
